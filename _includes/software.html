Software


All research breakthroughs achieved in GLOSE results in software components integrated into the GEMOC studio.

The GEMOC Studio brings techniques, frameworks, and environments together to facilitate the creation, integration, and automated processing of heterogeneous modeling languages. The studio is sustained as a research platform in an Eclipse project of the GEMOC Initiative.
